# Commercial Office Lease Agreement with Cityscape Realty

**Agreement ID:** LEASE-CTSCP-2021-001
**Parties:**
- **Tenant:** Innovatech Solutions Inc.
- **Landlord:** Cityscape Realty LLC.
**Lease Commencement Date:** August 1, 2021
**Lease Term:** Five (5) years, expiring July 31, 2026.

## 1. Leased Premises
Landlord leases to Tenant approximately **25,000 rentable square feet** of office space, designated as Suite 400, on the 4th floor of the building located at 123 Tech Way, San Francisco, CA 94105.

## 2. Rent
**2.1. Base Rent:** Base rent includes a 3% annual escalation clause.
    - Year 1: $1,875,000 annually
    - Year 5: $2,110,500 annually
**2.2. Additional Rent:** Tenant is responsible for its proportionate share (15.6%) of the building's annual operating expenses.

## 3. Renewal Option
Tenant has one (1) option to extend the lease term for an additional five (5) years. The rent for the renewal term will be set at 95% of the then-current Fair Market Value.
