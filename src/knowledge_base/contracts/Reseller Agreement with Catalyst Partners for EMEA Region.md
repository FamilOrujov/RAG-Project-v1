# Channel Partner Reseller Agreement with Catalyst Partners

**Agreement ID:** RES-CATLP-2023-007
**Parties:**
- **Company:** Innovatech Solutions Inc.
- **Reseller:** Catalyst Partners, a UK Limited Company.
**Effective Date:** September 5, 2023
**Term:** Two (2) years, with performance-based renewal options.

## 1. Appointment and Territory
Company hereby appoints Reseller as a non-exclusive authorized reseller of the **SynapseChat** platform ("the Product") in the European Union, the United Kingdom, and Switzerland (collectively, "EMEA").

## 2. Commission and Payment Structure
**2.1. Commission Tiers:** Reseller shall be entitled to a commission on the first-year ACV of all new business it closes. The commission is tiered based on the total ACV closed per fiscal year:
    - 0 - $500,000 USD: **20%** Commission
    - $500,001 - $1,500,000 USD: **25%** Commission
    - Above $1,500,000 USD: **30%** Commission
**2.2. Renewal Commission:** Reseller is entitled to a flat **10%** commission on all subsequent renewals for customers it originally signed.
