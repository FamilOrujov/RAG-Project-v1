# Cloud Services Agreement with Stratus Infrastructure

**Agreement ID:** CSA-STRTS-2022-004
**Parties:**
- **Client:** Innovatech Solutions Inc.
- **Vendor:** Stratus Infrastructure LLC.
**Effective Date:** July 1, 2022
**Term:** Three-year (36 months) committed term.

## 1. Scope of Services
Vendor will provide dedicated, single-tenant cloud hosting infrastructure and managed services for Innovatech's primary production environment. This agreement covers the provision of compute, storage, and networking necessary to operate the **NexusFlow** and **InsightIQ** platforms for Innovatech's customers in the North America region.

## 2. Service Level Agreement (SLA)
**2.1. Uptime Guarantee:** Vendor guarantees 99.99% monthly uptime for all provisioned services.
**2.2. Service Credits:** Should uptime fall below the guarantee, Vendor will issue service credits to Innovatech's account, ranging from 10% for <99.99% uptime to 100% for <95% uptime.

## 3. Financial Commitments
**3.1. Monthly Recurring Cost (MRC):** Innovatech commits to a minimum monthly spend of **One Hundred Twenty Thousand US Dollars ($120,000.00)** for the duration of the term. This includes the cost of pre-purchased 3-year reserved instances.
