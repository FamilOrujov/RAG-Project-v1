## Professional Summary
Seraphina is a highly creative and user-centric designer responsible for the overall user experience (UX) of the **NexusFlow** platform. She specializes in translating complex workflow automation concepts into intuitive, accessible, and elegant interfaces. Seraphina's work is critical to our product-led growth strategy, as the usability of NexusFlow is a key competitive differentiator.

## Key Responsibilities
- **End-to-End Design:** Lead the design process for major NexusFlow features, from initial user research and wireframing to high-fidelity mockups, prototyping, and final visual specifications.
- **User Research:** Plan and conduct user interviews, usability testing sessions, and surveys with key clients like **Momentum Machines** and **Silverline Logistics** to gather qualitative feedback and identify pain points.
- **Design System Management:** Act as a primary contributor to and advocate for the "Innovatech Design System," ensuring consistency, scalability, and a cohesive look and feel across all our products.
- **Collaboration:** Work in a triad with Product Managers and Engineering Leads to define feature requirements and ensure design feasibility. Collaborate closely with front-end engineers to implement designs to a high standard.

## Projects & Accomplishments
- **NexusFlow Visual Builder Redesign (2023):** Led the complete redesign of the drag-and-drop workflow builder, which resulted in a 30% reduction in the time required for new users to build their first workflow and a 15-point increase in our System Usability Scale (SUS) score.
- **Custom Connector UI Framework (2022):** Designed the standardized UI framework that allows customers and professional services teams to configure custom connectors, a feature heavily used by clients like **Silverline Logistics**.
- **Accessibility Initiative (Ongoing):** Championed and is currently leading the initiative to make the NexusFlow platform WCAG 2.1 AA compliant, opening up new opportunities in the public sector and large enterprise markets.
