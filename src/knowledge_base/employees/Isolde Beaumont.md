Isolde is a seasoned product leader who owns the strategic direction and roadmap for Innovatech's core application suite: **NexusFlow** and **InsightIQ**. She excels at synthesizing customer feedback, market trends, and technical possibilities into a clear, prioritized product vision. Isolde is the crucial link between our customers' needs and the solutions our engineering teams build.

## Key Responsibilities
- **Product Strategy & Roadmap:** Define and communicate the multi-quarter roadmap for both NexusFlow and InsightIQ, ensuring alignment with company goals and market opportunities.
- **Feature Prioritization:** Manage the product backlog, making data-informed decisions to prioritize features that deliver the most value to customers and the business.
- **Customer Feedback Loop:** Establish and manage processes for gathering feedback from customers, including leading the Customer Advisory Board which includes stakeholders from **Zenith Financial Group** and **Momentum Machines**.
- **Cross-Functional Leadership:** Lead a team of product managers and collaborate daily with engineering (Gideon Cross), design (Seraphina Jones), and product marketing (Silas Thorne) to bring products and features to market.
