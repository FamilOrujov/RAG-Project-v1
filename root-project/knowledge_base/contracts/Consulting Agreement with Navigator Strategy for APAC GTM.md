# Consulting Services Agreement with Navigator Strategy

**Agreement ID:** CSA-NAVGS-2023-003
**Parties:**
- **Client:** Innovatech Solutions Inc.
- **Consultant:** Navigator Strategy Group.
**Effective Date:** June 1, 2023
**Term:** Six (6) months.

## 1. Services
Consultant will provide strategic advisory services to assist Innovatech in developing a comprehensive go-to-market (GTM) strategy for the **NexusFlow** platform in the Asia-Pacific (APAC) region.

## 2. Key Deliverables
- **Phase 1: Market Analysis:** A detailed report on the competitive landscape and TAM.
- **Phase 2: GTM Strategy:** A strategic plan including pricing and channel strategy.
- **Phase 3: Operational Plan:** A roadmap for establishing a local presence.

## 3. Fees and Billing
Services will be billed on a time and materials basis at a blended rate of **$350.00 per hour**, not to exceed **$200,000.00**. Client shall own all IP of the deliverables.
