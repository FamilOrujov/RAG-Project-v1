# Master Service Agreement with Silverline Logistics

**Agreement ID:** MSA-SLVRL-2022-015
**Parties:**
- **Provider:** Innovatech Solutions Inc.
- **Client:** Silverline Logistics.
**Effective Date:** November 8, 2022
**Initial Term:** Thirty-six (36) months.

## 1. Scope of Services
Provider will deliver an enterprise-wide license to the **NexusFlow** platform for up to **one thousand (1,000) Authorized Users** across Client's supply chain management divisions.

## 2. Professional Services & Customization
**2.1. Implementation Package:** A one-time, fixed-fee package of **$150,000.00** which includes the development of three (3) custom API connectors for Silverline's WMS, SAP ERP, and the FourKites freight tracking platform.

## 3. Financial Terms
**3.1. Annual Subscription Fee (ACV):** Nine Hundred Thousand US Dollars ($900,000.00), payable in equal quarterly installments.
