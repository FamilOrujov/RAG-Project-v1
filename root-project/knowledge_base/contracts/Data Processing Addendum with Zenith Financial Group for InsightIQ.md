# Data Processing Addendum with Zenith Financial Group

**Agreement ID:** DPA-ZENTH-2023-002
**Parties:**
- **Data Controller:** Zenith Financial Group.
- **Data Processor:** Innovatech Solutions Inc.
**Effective Date:** May 20, 2023

## 1. Subject Matter
This Data Processing Addendum ("DPA") is incorporated into the Master Service Agreement (MSA-ZENTH-2023-001) and governs Innovatech's processing of Personal Data on behalf of Zenith Financial. The processing activities relate to the provision of the **InsightIQ** business analytics platform.

## 2. Sub-processors
Innovatech is authorized to use sub-processors, such as **Stratus Infrastructure LLC** for cloud hosting. Innovatech remains fully liable for the performance and compliance of its sub-processors.

## 3. Data Subject Rights & Breach Notification
Innovatech will assist Zenith in fulfilling its obligations to respond to data subject requests. In the event of a Personal Data Breach, Innovatech will notify Zenith without undue delay, and in no event later than 48 hours after becoming aware of the breach.
