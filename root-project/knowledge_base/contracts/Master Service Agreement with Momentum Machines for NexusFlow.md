# Master Service Agreement with Momentum Machines

**Agreement ID:** MSA-MOMTM-2023-001
**Parties:**
- **Provider:** Innovatech Solutions Inc., a Delaware corporation.
- **Client:** Momentum Machines Corp., a Michigan corporation.
**Effective Date:** January 15, 2023
**Initial Term:** Thirty-six (36) months.

## 1. Recitals
This Master Service Agreement ("MSA") governs the licensing of Provider's software and the provision of related services to the Client. Momentum Machines requires an enterprise-grade workflow automation solution to optimize its industrial manufacturing process, and Innovatech provides such a solution known as "NexusFlow".

## 2. License Grant and Services
**2.1. License:** Provider grants Client a non-exclusive, non-transferable, worldwide right to access and use the **NexusFlow** software for up to **five hundred (500) Authorized Users** for its internal business purposes.
**2.2. Support Services:** Provider will deliver its "Enterprise Support" package, including a dedicated Technical Account Manager (TAM), a 99.9% uptime SLA, and 24x7x365 support for critical issues (P1).

## 3. Financial Terms
**3.1. Annual Subscription Fee (ACV):** Four Hundred Eighty Thousand US Dollars ($480,000.00), billed annually in advance.
**3.2. Professional Services Fee:** A one-time fee of Fifty Thousand US Dollars ($50,000.00) for implementation and training.
