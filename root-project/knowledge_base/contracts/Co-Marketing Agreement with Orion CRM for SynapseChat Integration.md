# Co-Marketing Agreement with Orion CRM

**Agreement ID:** CMA-ORION-2023-008
**Parties:**
- Innovatech Solutions Inc. ("Innovatech")
- Orion CRM Inc. ("Orion")
**Effective Date:** October 1, 2023
**Term:** Twelve (12) months.

## 1. Purpose
This agreement outlines a collaborative marketing partnership to promote the official, certified integration between Innovatech's **SynapseChat** platform and Orion's "Orion Sales Cloud" product.

## 2. Joint Marketing Activities
- **Content Creation:** Co-author at least four (4) blog posts and one (1) whitepaper.
- **Webinars:** Host two (2) joint webinars.
- **Events:** Co-sponsor a booth at the TechGrowth 2024 conference.

## 3. Budget and Costs
The cost of the TechGrowth 2024 conference booth, estimated at **$30,000.00**, will be split equally (50/50). Lead attribution will be managed via a shared Salesforce campaign.
